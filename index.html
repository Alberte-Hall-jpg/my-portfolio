About the artist

Hello, I'm Alberte

I'm a landscape painter based in Denmark, inspired by the ever-changing light of Scandinavian nature — from golden summer meadows to misty autumn forests. Each painting is made by hand with care and intention.

I paint primarily in oils and acrylics, and I also take commissions — so if you have a special place or memory you'd love to see captured on canvas, I'd love to hear from you.
