Om kunstneren

Hej, jeg er Alberte

I'm a watercolour artist based in Denmark, drawn to the colours, light, and energy found in nature and everyday beauty. My paintings are loose, vivid, and full of life — I love the way watercolour moves and surprises you.

Each piece is painted by hand and made with genuine care. Whether you're looking for an original, a print for your wall, or a card for someone special — I hope my art brings a little more colour into your day.
